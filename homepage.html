Loan Application Rules

To determine your eligibility, the bank uses a credit worthiness scoring system. Please review the rules below carefully

  • If your account balance is greater than or equal to the loan amount +10 points otherwise -10 points.
  • A valid 6 months credit history +10 points.
  • If your last deposit was within 1 month +5 points.
  • If your last loan collection was more than 6 months ago +10 points.
  • If your loan repayment period is less than 6 months +5 points
  • If account type is Current +10 points, Savings +5 points

To qualify for a loan, you must score above 30 points.
